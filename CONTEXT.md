# Flowers & Coffee Shop — Project Context

## Core Concept
A modern flower shop combined with a coffee bar in **Prilep, Macedonia**. Differentiated by tech-forward operations (online ordering, delivery, digital payments) in a market where competitors lack even POS terminals.

## Target Services

### Core (Launch)
- **Flower Delivery** — primary revenue driver, targeting expats (US, Australia, Germany) who want to send flowers to family/friends in Prilep
- **Pre-made Bouquets** — arranged the night before, ready for walk-in purchase or next-day order
- **Flowers 2 Go / Coffee 2 Go** — combo concept: coffee + small flower bucket in the cup holder (cute, Instagrammable)
- **Decorations** — general decoration services
- **Events** — event flower arrangements

### Small In-Store Experience
- 2–3 tables for sit-down coffee
- Cozy, aesthetic environment

### Phase 2 (After Validation)
- **Team Building Workshops** — flower arranging workshops for corporate groups
- **Bachelorette Decorating** — custom decoration packages
- **Birthday Decorating** — custom decoration packages
- **Weddings** — full wedding flower/decoration services

## Key Differentiators
- Online ordering + delivery (competitors have zero digital presence)
- International payment processing for expat customers
- Website/app for remote ordering
- Modern POS system
- Coffee + flowers combo concept
- Pre-made bouquets for convenience

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Macedonian expats in US, Australia, Germany sending flowers to loved ones in Prilep
- **Secondary:** Local Prilep customers (walk-in, events, decorations)
