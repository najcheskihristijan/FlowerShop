# Flowers & Coffee Shop — Business Plan

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** April 2026
**Location:** Prilep, North Macedonia
**Status:** Pre-launch / Concept validation

---

## 1. Executive Summary

A modern flower-and-coffee shop in Prilep, Macedonia, built around a simple insight: **thousands of Macedonian expats want to send flowers to loved ones back home, but every flower shop in Prilep is stuck in the 1990s** — no websites, no online ordering, no card payments, no delivery tracking.

This business combines a small physical flower + coffee shop with a digital-first ordering platform targeting the diaspora in the US, Australia, and Germany. Revenue comes from three channels: online delivery orders (primary), walk-in flower and coffee sales, and event/decoration services.

**Starting budget:** EUR 5,000
**Business model:** Low-overhead, side hustle with growth potential
**Team:** Founder (operations, marketing, floristry — ~3h/day) + partner (shop operations, deliveries — ~12h/day)

---

## 2. Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Make sending flowers to Prilep as easy as ordering coffee — from anywhere in the world.

**Vision:** Become the first digitally-native flower brand in Prilep, and eventually expand to cover all major Macedonian cities for diaspora gifting.

## 4. Target Market

### Primary: Macedonian Diaspora (Online Revenue Driver)

- **Who:** Macedonian expats living in the US, Australia, and Germany
- **Why:** They want to send flowers for birthdays, name days (имендени), holidays, Mother's Day, 8 March, etc. Currently, they have to call a random florist, hope someone answers, arrange payment via bank transfer or asking a relative to pay cash
- **Size estimate:** Prilep municipality ~66,000 population. Significant diaspora — estimated 20,000–30,000 Prilep-origin expats in key markets
- **Key dates:** 8 March (Women's Day), Mother's Day, Easter, name days, Valentine's Day, New Year

### Secondary: Local Walk-in Customers

- **Who:** Prilep residents buying flowers for occasions or grabbing coffee
- **Why:** Convenience, modern experience, pre-made bouquets ready to go
- **Differentiator:** The coffee + flowers combo creates a daily-visit reason, not just occasion-based

### Tertiary: Event & Corporate Clients

- **Who:** Local businesses, event organizers, families planning celebrations
- **Why:** One-stop shop for decoration + flowers

## 5. Competitive Analysis

### Current Prilep Flower Shop Landscape

| Factor | Existing Shops | Our Shop |
|---|---|---|
| Online presence | None or basic Facebook page | Full website + social media |
| Online ordering | No | Yes — web/app |
| International payments | No | PayPal + card processing |
| Delivery | Informal / on request | Structured, trackable |
| POS terminal | No (cash only) | Yes |
| Coffee | No | Yes |
| Branding | Generic | Modern, Instagram-worthy |
| Operating hours | ~9 AM – 5 PM typical | 7 AM – 10 PM |
| Pre-made bouquets | Limited | Daily fresh arrangements |

### Competitive Advantage

1. **First-mover in digital** — no competitor has online ordering or international payments
2. **Extended hours** — 7 AM to 10 PM covers morning coffee crowd and evening last-minute buyers
3. **Dual revenue model** — coffee provides daily foot traffic; flowers provide high-margin occasion sales
4. **Diaspora lock-in** — once an expat uses the service successfully, switching cost is high (trust, saved addresses, reliability)
5. **Content-friendly concept** — the Flowers 2 Go combo is inherently shareable on social media

- **Primary:** Skopje wholesale flower market (weekly runs, 1.5h drive)
- **Frequency:** 2x per week minimum (Mon & Thu), more during peak seasons
- **Storage:** Small cooler/refrigeration unit in shop to extend freshness

- **Handled by:** Boyfriend (dedicated delivery person)
- **Area:** Prilep city and immediate surroundings
- **Vehicle:** Personal car (already available — no added cost)
- **Delivery fee:** 100–150 MKD local, 200–300 MKD outskirts
- **Same-day:** Orders placed before 12:00 noon
- **Next-day:** All other orders

## 9. Legal & Compliance

### Business Registration
- Register **DOOEL** (single-member LLC) at Central Registry of North Macedonia
- Estimated cost: ~EUR 150 (registration + notary fees)
- Required: Personal ID, founding act, bank account

### Licenses & Permits
- **Business activity code:** Retail sale of flowers and plants (NACE 47.76)
- **Food handling:** If serving coffee + pastries, need basic food safety compliance from Food & Veterinary Agency
- **Fiscal cash register:** Mandatory for all retail in MK — register with UJP (tax authority)
- **Music license:** If playing music in shop — ZAM (Здружение на авторите на Македонија)

### Tax Obligations
- **VAT:** Below MKD 2M annual revenue = VAT exempt (likely first year)
- **Profit tax:** 10% corporate tax
- **Personal income:** If drawing salary as founder
- **Monthly tax filings:** Via e-Tax system

### PayPal & International Payments
- PayPal Business account (registered in MK)
- Declare foreign currency income to bank/tax authority
- Currency conversion: PayPal → EUR → MKD

## 10. Risk Analysis

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low initial demand | Medium | High | Pre-launch marketing, soft opening, leverage personal network |
| Flower spoilage / waste | Medium | Medium | Start with small inventory, make-to-order where possible, pre-made only what sells |
| Seasonal revenue swings | High | Medium | Coffee provides baseline daily revenue; push events in slow periods |
| Competitor copies digital approach | Low (short-term) | Medium | First-mover advantage, build brand loyalty fast, superior UX |
| Boyfriend unavailable for delivery | Medium | High | Build relationship with a backup driver; consider Wolt/Glovo later |
| Boyfriend burnout (12h/day) | Medium | High | Hire part-time help once profitable; adjust hours if needed |
| PayPal account restrictions | Low | High | Maintain clean transaction history, consider adding Stripe as backup |
| Rent increase / location loss | Low | Medium | Start with flexible lease terms if possible |
