Sardoniks SD

One of the largest flower importers in Macedonia

Weekly imports from the Aalsmeer flower auction (Netherlands)

The Aalsmeer auction sources from Colombia, Ecuador, Kenya, Israel, Turkey, and other growing regions. This is the standard supply chain for most Macedonian florists.

Peak Sales Periods & Popular Flowers

Key Occasions (by Revenue Impact)

  • 8 March — Women's Day — Biggest flower day in MK. 5–10x normal sales. Roses & carnations.
  • 14 Feb — Valentine's Day — 3–5x normal. Red roses dominate.
  • Mother's Day — 3–5x normal. Mixed bouquets popular.
  • Name Days (Именден) — Steady demand year-round. Culturally very important.
  • Easter (Велигден) — 2–3x normal. Spring flowers.
  • Wedding Season (Jun–Sep) — Sustained uplift. Bride bouquets + venue decoration.
  • New Year / Christmas — 2x normal. Arrangements & potted plants.
  • Funerals — Year-round baseline. Wreaths & arrangements.

Key Takeaways

  1. 23 flower shops in Prilep, zero with online ordering or e-commerce — wide open digital gap
  2. International delivery services already serve Prilep — but via anonymous local partners with no transparency. A branded local shop can undercut them on price and beat them on trust.
  3. Typical bouquet range: 900–2,500 MKD (EUR 15–40) — this is the sweet spot for everyday orders
  4. 8 March is the Super Bowl of flowers in MK — plan inventory and marketing around it heavily
  5. Sardoniks SD is the main wholesale supplier — imported from Dutch auction, standard for all MK florists
  6. Price sensitivity is real — many customers buy a single rose. The Coffee 2 Go combo (250–400 MKD) hits the right impulse-buy price point.
  7. Gardenia is the only competitor with serious Facebook presence (16.6K likes) — but no website, no online ordering, no payments
